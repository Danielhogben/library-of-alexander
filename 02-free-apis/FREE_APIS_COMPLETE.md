## 🔑 Key Tips

1. **OpenRouter is the best starting point.** 50+ free models, OpenAI-compatible API, no signup for basic use.

2. **Groq is the fastest free API.** Llama 4 Scout at 200+ tokens/sec, 1000 req/day free.

3. **Cerebras is the most generous.** 14.4K req/day, 1M tokens/day, GPT-OSS-120B for free.

4. **Google AI Studio gives you Gemini 2.5 Pro free.** 20 req/day, 250K tokens/min. Best for vision tasks.

5. **For coding: Qwen Code + Qwen3-Coder-480B.** 2000 req/day free via Qwen OAuth.

6. **For local: Ollama + Open WebUI.** Best UX for running models locally.

7. **For production: vLLM + free tier APIs.** vLLM for self-hosted, free APIs for prototyping.

8. **Stack multiple free APIs.** Use OpenRouter as a fallback, Groq for speed, Cerebras for volume.
